Achievement Watcher A sexy achievement file parser with real-time notification.

View every achievement earned on your PC
with highly customizable notification
whether it's coming from Steam, a Steam emulator, and more.

Download
Windows 7 - 10 64bits

The original idea behind this app was that some steam emulators generate a text file where your unlocked achievements are stored. But they aren't very friendly to know which is which, until now.

  • Simple & Modern UI

    The UI was made to be very simple while keeping a clean layout. On the first launch, the app will build the cache for all your games. This process can take a while if you have a lot of them but don't worry : after that you're ready to go and it will load pretty fast.

  • Achievement notification

    Display highly customizable notification when you unlock an achievement. Change the sound, take a screenshot, make your gamepad rumble, notify on progress, choose what to display and how.

  • Unified Experience

    By unifying as best as possible all your different achievements source and fixing their respective quirk. You can enjoy your achievements in a streamlined interface and experience no matter where they come from.

  • Large compatibility

    From official Steam client, to various Steam emulators, and even RPCS3 Sony PlayStation 3 emulator. This is an on-going endeavor but you can check the full list of what this app can import.

  • Tons of options

    From filters and sorting options, to language choice, to view hidden achievements, to merging duplicates, to notification, to achievements sources, to blacklisting, there are plenty of settings to play around with.
